Welcome to Book Inventory Management System

This website is a comprehensive solution for managing your book inventory effectively. It allows users to:

Add new books: Easily add new books to your collection, including title, author, genre, and availability status.
Search and filter: Quickly search for books by title or filter by genre to find what you're looking for.
Manage inventory: Edit, update, and remove books from the inventory with ease.
View all books: Browse all available books, complete with detailed information and cover images.

Our Book Inventory Management System aims to simplify the process of organizing, tracking, and managing a library or personal book collection.
